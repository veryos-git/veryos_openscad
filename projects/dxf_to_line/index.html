DXF → OpenSCAD constant-width wall

Upload a DXF with one or two closed 2D loops. With one loop, the output is n_rings concentric walls shrunk inward by margin + line_width each step. With two loops, the output morphs point-by-point from the smaller loop (innermost ring) to the larger loop (outermost ring). All dimensions in millimetres.
